Steam Distribution Network Overview

The steam distribution network efficiently delivers high-pressure steam from the central boiler to multiple industrial processes. The system includes pressure control valves, flow meters, and condensate recovery to maximize energy efficiency and minimize waste.

Key Components:

  • Main Header: Primary steam distribution line from boiler
  • Control Valves: Regulate steam flow and pressure to each process
  • Flow Meters: Monitor steam consumption and efficiency
  • Condensate Return: Recover and recycle condensed steam back to boiler
  • Process Connections: Individual steam supplies for heating, power, and drying

Benefits: Reduced energy costs, improved process control, enhanced safety, and sustainable steam utilization across the facility.
